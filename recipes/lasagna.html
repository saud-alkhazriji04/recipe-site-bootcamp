Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Prep time: 30 minutes  |  Cook time: 1 hour

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and lay flat on a baking sheet.
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef and onion until the beef is browned. Add the garlic and cook for 1 more minute. Drain excess fat.
  3. Stir the marinara sauce into the beef mixture. Simmer for 5 minutes, then remove from heat.
  4. In a bowl, combine the ricotta, egg, parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  5. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Layer 3 noodles, a third of the ricotta mixture, a third of the meat sauce, and a third of the mozzarella. Repeat layers twice more.
  6. Top with the remaining mozzarella and the Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  7. Let stand for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
