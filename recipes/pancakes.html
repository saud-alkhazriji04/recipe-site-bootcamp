Majboos Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice, rinsed and soaked for 30 minutes
  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely sliced
  • 3 tomatoes, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro and fried onions to garnish

Prep time: 40 minutes  |  Cook time: 1 hour

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large heavy pot over medium-high heat. Fry the sliced onions until golden brown. Remove half the onions and set aside for garnish.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the pot and brown on all sides for about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the garlic, turmeric, cumin, coriander, cinnamon, and black pepper. Stir to coat the chicken with the spices and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add the diced tomatoes and cook until they soften, about 5 minutes. Season with salt.
  5. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes until the chicken is cooked through.
  6. Remove the chicken pieces and set aside. Drain the rice and add it to the broth in the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to the lowest setting, cover tightly, and cook for 20 minutes.
  7. Return the chicken to the pot on top of the rice for the last 5 minutes to reheat. Serve on a large platter garnished with fried onions and fresh cilantro.
